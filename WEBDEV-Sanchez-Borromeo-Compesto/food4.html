Ingredients

  • 8 ounces kalabasa
  • 1 piece carrot
  • 3/4 cup malunggay leaves
  • 1 cup cooking oil

    • Batter

  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 4 grams Maggi Magic Sarap
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup water

    • Dipping Sauce

  • ½ piece onion chopped
  • 2 tablespoons green onion chopped
  • ¾ cups white vinegar
  • 4 grams Maggi Magic Sarap

Instructions

    Slice the kalabasa and carrot into matchstick pieces.
    Combine all the sliced vegetables including the malunggay leaves in a mixing bowl. Toss to blend.
    Make the batter by beating an egg on a separate bowl. Add water. Mix well. Gradually add flour and cornstarch while mixing. Add Maggi Magic Sarap. Whisk until the texture of the batter smoothens.
    Combine the batter with the vegetables. Fold until the vegetables are completely coated with the batter.
    Make the dipping sauce by combining all dipping sauce ingredient in a bowl. Stir until blended.
    Heat oil in a pan. Scoop around ¼ cup of mixture and place it on a saucer or small bowl. Slide the mixture into the pan. Fry until golden brown and crispy.
    Put-in the cabbage. Add salt to taste. Stir. Cover and continue to cook for 3 minutes.
    Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve with a spicy fish sauce dipping sauce.
