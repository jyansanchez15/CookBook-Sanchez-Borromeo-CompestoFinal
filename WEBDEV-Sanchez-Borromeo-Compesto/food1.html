Ingredients

  • 1 can corned beef
  • 1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbs. olive oil
  • ½ tsp. cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp. smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp. fresh ground black pepper

Instructions

    In a large pan over medium heat, add olive oil and onions.
    Sauté onions until translucent.
    Add garlic, cook till fragrant, about 30 seconds.
    Add tomatoes and juices, cayenne, paprika, and black pepper, and mix.
    Turn heat to medium high and reduce liquid, stirring occasionally to prevent the bottom from burning.
    Add corned beef and take a fork and smash the meat.
    Mix all ingredients.
    Serve warm with garlic fried rice and egg.
